It sounds like your baby is experiencing the common 4-month sleep regression. This is actually a positive sign of development as their sleep cycles mature and become more like adults.

Tips to manage this phase:

  • Maintain a consistent bedtime routine (bath, book, bed).
  • Ensure the sleep environment is dark, cool, and quiet.
  • Practice 'drowsy but awake' transitions to encourage self-soothing.
  • Monitor wake windows carefully (usually 1.5–2 hours at this age).
